Zero Standing Privilege means no permanent privileged credentials exist in your system. Access rights are granted only when specifically requested and approved, with temporary credentials that automatically expire. This reduces your attack surface by up to 97% compared to traditional always-on access.

ArgusPAM combines enterprise-grade security with accessibility. Unlike traditional solutions that can cost hundreds of thousands of dollars and require extensive expertise to deploy, ArgusPAM is open source, AI-driven, and can be deployed in minutes. It's designed specifically for database access with Zero Standing Privilege principles at its core.
