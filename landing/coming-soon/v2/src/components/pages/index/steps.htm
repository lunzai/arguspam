How it works

Smart access workflow with AI oversight

From request to post-session audit, ArgusPAM automates the entire privileged access lifecycle with AI-powered risk assessment at every critical step.

1. Request & Instant AI Analysis

Your team requests database access in seconds—select the database, set a time window, and explain why. Our AI immediately evaluates every request for risk, delivering a clear assessment in under 10 seconds. No more waiting hours for security reviews or approvals stuck in limbo.

2. Smart Approval Decisions

Approvers see the full picture: what's being requested, why it's needed, and an AI-powered risk score that highlights potential concerns. Make informed decisions fast—approve with confidence, reject with clarity, or request more context. No more blind trust or endless email chains.

3. Secure Access, Zero Hassle

Once approved, one click activates your session. ArgusPAM instantly creates temporary credentials with precisely the permissions you need—nothing more, nothing less. Get secure, encrypted connection details and start working immediately. Enterprise security that feels effortless.

4. Auto-Cleanup with AI Watchdog

When your session ends, ArgusPAM automatically destroys all temporary credentials—no orphaned accounts, no forgotten access. Simultaneously, AI reviews every query you ran, checking for anything unusual or out of scope. Complete lifecycle security that never sleeps or forgets.

5. Intelligent Alerts, Not Noise

Only get notified when it matters. Low-risk sessions? Optional review. High-risk activity detected? Security team gets immediate alerts with full context. Cut through the noise and respond to real threats in minutes, not days. Your security team stays focused on what's critical.
